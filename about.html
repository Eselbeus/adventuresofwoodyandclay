The Background Story

A Catan Comic

The original 50-episode series centers around the characters Woody and Clay. Neither of them are particularly intelligent charachters and each is totally infatuated by the resource that they represent: For Woody, that resourse is wood, and for Clay that resourse is clay. They join forces to some degree with a character called Catanman, who apparently has some superpowers. One would assume he can fly since he has a cape. He is also a bit of a pothead and therefore his favorite resource is sheep, because well...they dwell in the grass
