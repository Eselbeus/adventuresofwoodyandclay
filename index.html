Adventures of Woody and Clay

A Catan Comic

Adventures of Woody and Clay began as a notorious stick-figure comic on an Angelfire page back in 2002. The comic is themed around the board game, Catan. This official site for the comic will provide a new home for the series as well as updates for potential new adventures.

The Original Comic

  • Adventure 1: The First Tour of Catanworld (Episodes 1-10)
  • Adventure 2: The Destruction of Catanworld (Episodes 11-20)
  • Adventure 3: The Creation of Catanworld (Episodes 21-30)
  • Adventure 4: The Council of Catanworld (Episodes 31-40)
  • Adventure 5: The Second Tour of Catanworld (Episodes 41-50)
